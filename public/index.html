What this system does

HVAC AI Intake System acts as an always-on front office for HVAC contractors. It answers inbound texts, calls, and web chats, understands what a customer needs, and captures the details a dispatcher needs to book the job — all without a human having to pick up the phone first.

Every conversation is screened by a built-in safety gate for emergencies (like gas leaks or carbon monoxide) and escalated immediately to a live dispatcher when needed. Everything else is queued, classified, and handed off cleanly for scheduling.
